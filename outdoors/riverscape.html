Riverscape Metropark

Hours:

24 hours a day, 365 days a year

Visit:

Official Site

Dayton's Favorite Festival Grounds

Beautifully landscapped gardens, fountains, flowers, and of course the Great Miami River are just some of the lovely sights to see at Riverscape Metropark. Enjoy a stroll, or maybe a paddleboat ride, and then grab a bite to eat at the Riverscape Cafe. Enjoy events throughout the year such as the Celtic Festival, River Run, and R&B concerts. There's always something to do at Riverscape!

Festival Grounds

Check out a concert or festival! Riverscape metropark is home to a covered pavillion and a large fairgrounds for food truck festivals, music festivals, and culture festivals throughout the year. Keep an eye on our events page for all upcoming events.

Paddlesport Rentals

Come enjoy a day of paddling in the Great Miami River! Riverscape is a great location for first-time and beginner paddlers of 8 years or older. Paddlesport rentals run on select dates June through August. Please view the official site for safety details and requirements.

Ice Rink

Visit in the winter months to enjoy the region's largest outdoor ice rink! It's suitable for all ages and includes programs such as lessons, curling, and other activities. You can also visit the concession area for a cup of hot cocoa or a soft pretzel. Visit the official site for hours and admission.
