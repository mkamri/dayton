Discover Native American Culture

Immerse yourself in this 800-year-old reconstructed village featuring structures that have been rebuilt in their original locations. Learn about the Fort Ancient culture, social practices, diet, and more as you explore the structures and the central indoor Interpretive Center. Great for adults and kids alike!

Settlements

Experience the Indian Village just as it was hundreds of years ago. Settlements have been recreated exactly as they were, creating a hands-on immersive experience that will leave a great impression on every member of the family.

Open-air

Sunwatch has an open-air museum facility in a natural setting. Whether you're a lover of nature or interested in the true historical feeling, you'll enjoy a breath of fresh air as you walk the paths of Fort Ancient history and tradition.

Lifestyle Exhibits

Inside the main exhibit hall, you can view exhibits on all aspects of Fort Ancient life, including diet, social gatherings, and rituals. Discover what made the Ohioan Fort Ancient Native Americans different from others!
