A Hidden Oasis

Smith Memorial Gardens may be less than one acre, but it is a hidden gem with no detail left untouched. This garden is pruned and prepared meticulously for every season, sporting seasonal changes from lovely spring flowers to fall foliage. The garden house is home to activities such as the Perennial Sale every April, and can be rented for weddings and small parties.

Summer Storytime

Enjoy a cozy storytime atmosphere at Smith Gardens. Bring your little ones and curl up to listen to a story read by a volunteer from the local Wright Public Library. Call ahead to find out what book will be read this week!

Seasonal Changes

Smith hires a team of gardeners to care for the flowers, trees, and bushes. Witness butterflies in the spring and summer, and critters in the fall and winter. Watch as the flowers bloom and the trees fade from green to red. There's always something new to enjoy!

Private Events

Smith Gardens is the perfect location for private events such as weddings, birthday parties, bridal and baby showers, and more. Smith Gardens preserves a quiet, cozy atmosphere for small get-togethers and intimate events. Call ahead to reserve your date!
